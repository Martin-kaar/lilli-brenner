Song cover design

The task
We had to choose 8 songs from different genres before we knew our task, and afterwards had to create 8 covers.

My interpretation
I first started with sketching ruffly how the music made me feel and then creating moodboards about the atmosphere of the songs. After combining those aspects I started designing the covers with the intention of trying different styles and tools to represent the songs.
